*****CRICKET*****

Rules:

  • Teams: A cricket match is played between two teams, each consisting of 11 players.
  • Objective: The team with the most runs at the end of the match wins.
  • Overs: An over consists of 6 legal deliveries bowled by a bowler.
  • Innings: Each team gets one or two innings to bat, depending on the match format.
  • Pitch Dimensions: The pitch is 22 yards long and 10 feet wide.
