*****FOOTBALL*****

Rules

  • Team Composition: Each team consists of 11 players, including 1 goalkeeper.
  • Objective: Score more goals than the opposing team by getting the ball into their net.
  • Field Dimensions: The pitch should be between 100-130 yards long and 50-100 yards wide.
  • Time: Two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • Kick-off: The match begins with a kick-off from the center circle.
