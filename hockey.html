*****HOCKEY*****

Rules

  • Team Composition: Each team has 11 players, including a goalkeeper.
  • Objective: Score more goals than the opponent by hitting the ball into their net.
  • Field Dimensions: The field is 100 yards long and 60 yards wide, with a goal at each end.
  • Start of Play: The match begins with a pass from the center of the field.
  • Restart After a Goal: The team that conceded the goal takes a center pass.
